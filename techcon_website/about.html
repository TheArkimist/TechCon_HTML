TechCon exists to inspire, educate, and connect people passionate about technology. Our mission is to empower innovation through community-driven events, hands-on experiences, and thought-provoking discussions on emerging technologies.

Notable Past Speakers

Dr. Aisha Rahman

A leading AI researcher and professor, Dr. Rahman has pioneered ethical machine learning practices. Her keynote on "Responsible AI" at TechCon 2022 sparked important conversations on bias in algorithms.

Javier Torres

CEO of GreenTech Labs, Javier Torres is known for his work in sustainable tech solutions. His session, "Tech for a Greener Tomorrow," highlighted real-world applications of renewable technologies.

Mei Lin Zhang

Cybersecurity strategist Mei Lin Zhang brought attention to the growing risks in digital infrastructure with her impactful talk, "Guardians of the Net," at TechCon 2019.
